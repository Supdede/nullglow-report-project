Core Doctrine

This section details the fundamental beliefs of the cult. It covers the nature of their central entity, the "Nullglow," and the "Heartbeat" rhythm that forms the basis of their rituals. Below, a visual flow represents how these concepts are believed to interact.

Doctrinal Concepts

A. Nature of the Nullglow

  • Definition: Not a god, but the unseen, corrosive residue of all suffering, injustice, and failure. It is the purest distillation of malice—the "dark radiation" of reality's inherent cruelty.
  • The Un-Light: Referred to as "light from the absence," it strips all meaning, hope, and purpose from those who perceive its presence.
  • Purpose: The Nullglow's sole purpose is the *dissolution* of all meaningful existence, leaving behind only the void.

B. The "Heartbeat"

  • The Rhythm of Decay: A constant, slow, and dreadful cosmic rhythm. Cult members believe this is the slow, inevitable march of all things toward decay and oblivion.
  • Observational Data: This rhythm is the basis for their key rituals, which rely on repetitive, low-frequency sounds (drumming) to mimic the supposed pulse.

Purpose and Practices

This section outlines the primary activities of the cult. These practices are not arbitrary worship but are believed to be active mechanisms for assisting the Nullglow in its goal of cosmic dissolution.

A. The Ritual Drumming

  • Primary Practice: The most significant and defining practice. This is not simply worship, but an active mechanism.
  • Mechanism of Action (Belief): By perfectly reproducing the rhythm, cultists believe they are resonating with the cosmic malice, drawing the Nullglow's focus, and feeding its strength.
  • The Sound: Typically slow, deep, monotonous, and extremely hypnotic, intended to instill a sense of dread and inescapable fate.

B. The Collection of "Souls"

  • Definition of a "Soul": The vital energy, purpose, and will of a person—the intangible essence that gives life meaning.
  • The Offering: The Nullglow cannot consume raw matter; it only feeds on meaning and vitality.
  • The Act: A violent, orchestrated act of taking a life or inducing immense psychological destruction. They view this as a cosmic necessity, not a criminal act.

The Embodiment: The "Conduit"

This section addresses speculation regarding a key figure, a leader who may believe they are a physical manifestation of the Nullglow's will. This individual is referred to as "The Conduit" or "The Vessel."

Identity: The Conduit / The Vessel

Role: This leader does not claim to *be* the Nullglow, but rather its **active, physical manifestation** in the material world. They see themselves as the necessary bridge between cosmic malice and human action.

Suspected Personality Profile:

Emotional Sterility

Believes they are permanently infused with the Nullglow's essence, making them perpetually cold and incapable of true human empathy.

Mandate

Primary role is to orchestrate the largest and most effective "collections," acting as the Nullglow's literal hand and strategic mind. A force of profound, dedicated nihilism.

Utterance

Often speaks of the futility of all life and the inherent *rightness* of decay and death, viewing all constructive human endeavor as a pathetic delay of the inevitable.
